About Us

Our Team

Eco Escape Plan was founded by a group of friends who had always dreamed of living off the grid and reducing their carbon footprint. They were all passionate about sustainability, nature, and the environment and wanted to create a platform to help others achieve their off-grid living goals. The founders met while they were all studying Environmental Science at the University of Guelph. They spent hours discussing their shared vision of a world where people can live in harmony with nature. After graduation, they went their separate ways, but they stayed in touch and continued to work on sustainability projects together. One day, while they were all gathered for a reunion, they decided to make their off-grid dreams a reality. They pooled their resources and purchased a piece of land in a remote area, far from the hustle and bustle of city life. They built their own eco-friendly homes, using sustainable materials and renewable energy sources, and started living off the land. As they settled into their new lifestyle, they realized that many shared their dream of living off-grid but didn't know where to start. They decided to create a website, Eco Escape Plan, to share their knowledge and experience with others. They wanted to create a community of like-minded people who could support each other on their off-grid journey. Today, Eco Escape Plan is a thriving online community with thousands of members from around the world. The website offers practical advice on everything from building your own home to growing your own food and raising animals. The founders continue to be actively involved, sharing their own experiences and inspiring others to live sustainably.
